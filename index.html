Our Story

First Meeting

October 2018 - We met at a coffee shop when Michael accidentally spilled his drink on Monica's book. Instead of getting angry, she laughed and we talked for hours.

First Date

November 2018 - Our first official date was at the same coffee shop, this time with no spills! We discovered our shared love for hiking and indie music.

Moving In Together

June 2020 - After two years of dating, we took the big step of moving in together. Our apartment quickly became filled with plants, books, and laughter.

First Vacation

August 2020 - Our first trip together to the mountains where we discovered our shared love for adventure and nature.

First Holidays

December 2020 - Our first holiday season together, creating new traditions and blending our families.

The Proposal

October 2021 - On our 3-year anniversary, Michael recreated our first date at that same coffee shop. He got down on one knee and asked Monica to be his wife.
